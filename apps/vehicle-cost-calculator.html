What it does

Know the real cost
of every journey.

Build a quick manual estimate or connect a supported online routing provider. Vehicle profiles, measured fill-up consumption, saved journeys, and backups stay in this browser unless you export them yourself.

✓ Petrol, diesel, LPG, hybrid, plug-in hybrid, and electric vehicles
✓ One-way, return, custom-multiplier, and extra-distance journeys
✓ Automatic route distance and toll estimates where configured and supported
✓ Manual distance and toll fallback at all times
✓ Fill-up tracking for measured older-vehicle consumption
✓ Parking, ferry, maintenance, and custom expenses
✓ Cost per passenger, printable summaries, and local exports
✓ No account, analytics, advertising, or telemetry

Privacy by default

Manual calculations run entirely in your browser. Vehicles, fill-ups, and saved journeys remain on this device. If you enable online routing, only the route locations, stops, and selected route options are sent to the configured provider through the secure proxy; provider coverage and privacy terms apply. Automatic toll prices are estimates and are not available for every road or country.
